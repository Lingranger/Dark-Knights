Origins and Evolution

The game of darts has a rich and fascinating history. What began as a pastime for soldiers in medieval England has evolved into a highly competitive professional sport. Darts was originally played with arrows and the bottom of wine barrels. Over the centuries, it transformed into the organized game we know today, with standardized rules and international tournaments.
